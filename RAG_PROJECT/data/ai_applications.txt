AI is used across many industries and daily-life applications.

Healthcare: AI helps in medical imaging, disease prediction, and personalized treatment recommendations.

Finance: AI is used for fraud detection, credit scoring, risk management, and algorithmic trading.

Education: AI powers adaptive learning platforms, automated grading, and tutoring assistants.

Transportation: AI supports self-driving technologies, traffic prediction, and route optimization.

Customer Service: chatbots and virtual assistants provide 24/7 support and faster responses.

Manufacturing: AI enables predictive maintenance, quality inspection, and robotic automation.
